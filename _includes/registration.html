Registration Procedure

Please follow the steps below to complete your registration.

  1. EACSL membership.
    EACSL membership is mandatory for CSL registration.
    It is not required if you attend workshops only.
    Membership must be obtained directly as described on the EACSL website: https://www.eacsl.org/membership/
  2. Pre-registration.
    All participants (CSL and/or workshops) must pre-register using the following website: https://dr01.azur-colloque.fr/inscription/en
  3. Validation.
    Once your pre-registration has been validated, you will be able to proceed with payment.
  4. Payment.
    After validation, select the appropriate registration fee and pay online by credit card.
