Registration Procedure

Please follow the steps below to complete your registration.

  1. EACSL membership.
    EACSL membership is mandatory for CSL registration.
    It is not required, but it is welcome, if you attend workshops only.
    EACSL membership (€20 for regular members and €8 for students) can be obtained with CSL or workshop registration, or directly from EACSL, as described on the EACSL website: https://www.eacsl.org/membership/
  2. Pre-registration.
    All participants (CSL and/or workshops) must pre-register using the following website: https://dr01.azur-colloque.fr/inscription/en

    Note: If you have already pre-registered but have not yet paid the EACSL membership, and you prefer to pay it together with CSL or workshop registration, you can cancel your pre-registration and choose a new category that includes it.
    The new fee categories including EACSL membership were introduced on the morning of 15 January. If the registration site is not working, please wait, as the update may take a few hours to be deployed.

  3. Validation.
    Once your pre-registration has been validated, you will be able to proceed with payment.
  4. Payment.
    After validation, select the appropriate registration fee and pay online by credit card (or, for French institutions only, by bank transfer).
