Social Dinner

The social dinner is included with all CSL registrations.

The social dinner will start at 7:30 PM on Wednesday, February 25, and will be held at:

Le 220 – Maison Internationale d'Accueil Saint-François d'Assise
220 rue de la Convention
75015 Paris

Please keep your badge with you, as it will be required for entry.

The venue is near the Convention metro station on line 12, the same line that serves the Front Populaire metro station, located in front of the Conference Center.

Within walking distance of the dinner venue is also the Félix Faure metro station on line 8.

N.B. Unfortunately, due to maintenance work, line 12 will be completely closed starting at 10:00 PM on Wednesday, February 25. Therefore, if you plan to use this line for your return, please make sure to arrive at the Convention station before 10:00 PM.
